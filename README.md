# 🌳 Save Ooo
### A Campaign for the Ending Finn & Jake Deserve

---

## 📖 About the Campaign

**Save Ooo** is a fan-driven campaign fighting for a **healing ending** to the world of Adventure Time.

We believe Finn and Jake deserved to grow old together in Ooo — not in death.  
Simon deserved to be saved, not left as a ghost.  
Betty's love deserved to transform the cursed crown into a **crown of healing**.

> *"I, like Betty herself, am willing to do anything to keep this home warmer and brighter."*

---

## 🎯 Campaign Goals

- **Cancel "Side Quests"** — an irrelevant prequel that ignores the real story
- **Return to the Season 10 Finale** — where the real story ended
- **Change the ending** — give Finn & Jake the healing finale they earned
- **Create Season 11** — because the world of Ooo needs more episodes

---

## 📣 Your Voice Matters

Every fan can **share their story** with the campaign.  
We are collecting real stories from fans to show how much the original ending hurt us — and why we deserve a better one.

---

## ✨ Why This Campaign?

- **The original ending was a betrayal** — Finn and Jake ended together, but not in life — in death.
- **Ooo is not just a cartoon** — for many of us, Ooo was a home we grew up in.
- **We have the right to ask for a better ending** — an ending our heroes earned.

---
